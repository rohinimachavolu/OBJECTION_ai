Fair Labor Standards Act - Overtime Pay
Citation: 29 USC ยง207
Jurisdiction: Federal
Category: Employment

Employees covered by the Fair Labor Standards Act must receive overtime pay for hours worked over 40 in a workweek at a rate not less than time and one-half their regular rates of pay.

Exemptions: Executive, administrative, professional, outside sales employees, and certain computer employees are exempt from overtime requirements.

Plain English: If you work more than 40 hours per week, your employer must pay you 1.5 times your regular hourly rate for the extra hours.
